Welcome to the XXIX Convencion de FerroModelistas y Ferroaficionados de la Republica Mexicana - Hermosillo 2026

Every year a convention is held in selected cities in the Mexican Republic and this year it is in Hermosillo, Sonora! You will have the opportunity to learn another side of Mexican History. Learn about the Impact that Railroads had in the state of Sonora! From the pioneering days of the Sonora Railway to the Sud Pacifico de Mexico to Ferrocarril Del Pacifico, Ferrocarril Sonora Baja California to Ferromex. Learn how mining and revolution shaped Sonora History


Poster Submissions

Do you have a passion for Railroads? Do you like Railroad History? Are you a Railfan? A Modeler? Want to Participate? The Convention is looking for participants to submit posters to be presented in the exhibit hall! Please contact us to learn more to follow our guidelines
